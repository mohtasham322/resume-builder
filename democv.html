How to List Down Skills in CV

When writing a skills list in your CV, it's essential to include relevant skills that showcase your qualifications and suitability for the desired position. Here's how to present your skills effectively:

  1. Identify key skills: Review the job description and identify the skills that are specifically mentioned or required for the role.
  2. Tailor to the position: Select skills that align with the job requirements and the industry you're targeting. Focus on both technical and transferable skills.
  3. Be specific: Instead of using generic terms, provide specific examples of how you have applied those skills in previous roles or projects.
  4. Group similar skills: Group related skills under subheadings such as technical skills, soft skills, or industry-specific skills.

Skills Examples

Technical Skills:
  • Programming languages (e.g., Python, Java)
  • CAD software proficiency (e.g., AutoCAD, Revit)
  • Structural analysis and design
  • Project management
  • Site surveying and measurements
Soft Skills:
  • Communication and interpersonal skills
  • Problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Adaptability and flexibility
  • Time management and organizational skills
Industry-Specific Skills:
  • Knowledge of building codes and regulations
  • Construction project planning and scheduling
  • Cost estimation and budgeting
  • Risk assessment and mitigation
  • Quality control and assurance
